Rules:

  1. Get as many Berries as you can! (colored circle)

  3. You lose if you hit the Shrubs! (black squares)

Modes:

  • Manual: You have to play and win!
  • Automatic: The game plays itself (tries to!)

Difficulty:

  • Easy: Get to 300 points as fast as you can and watch out for obstacles.
  • Hard: Same goal as Easy Mode but the Shrubs expand faster and the Berries last shorter in the map.
